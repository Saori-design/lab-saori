My Story

Hello, everyone. I’m Saori(I'm Japanese). I have been living in Canada for 3 years. The reason why I’m learning interaction design at George Brown College is to become a UI/UX designer. I went to Osaka university of Arts, and Keio University law school in Japan. But I don’t have carrier for a design industry.(only worked for design jobs as intern and individually) When I was 25, I started to ask myself what do I really want to do in this life. Becoming a designer was my dream when I was small and I decided to leave the last job(It was a transportation company) and learn design again in Canada. Nowadays, digital device has been sprayed with great speed in the world, and users feel happy with a good experience design from websites and mobile apps.

Good designer/Good developer can answer the high demands and create an incredible idea through the UI and UX, I shifted to become UI/UX designer. For now, learning the theory of user experience is interesting. The reason why I chose Canada to learn design is I am attracted by international cultures, beautiful nature, and warm people. I love meeting new people, trying new things (new technology, trended things, etc). My personality is ambitious and enthusiastic to learn new skills.
